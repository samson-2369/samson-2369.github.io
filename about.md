# About Norris Cornell

I am a cybersecurity professional with experience spanning identity and access management, cyber defense operations, and critical infrastructure security.

My work and research focus on understanding how trust, visibility, and system dependencies create both defensive opportunities and attack surface across modern environments.

## Professional Focus

I work across cybersecurity domains including IAM, blue team operations, and operational technology security, with an emphasis on the systems organizations rely on every day.

## Industrial / SCADA Interest

My academic and technical interests include industrial control system (ICS) and SCADA security, industrial resilience, asset visibility, cyber hygiene, and the real‑world security challenges of critical infrastructure.

## Research & Communication

I use writing, presentations, and technical projects to translate complex cybersecurity topics into practical insights for defenders, engineers, and decision‑makers.

## Areas of Interest

- Identity and Access Management (IAM)
- Industrial control systems, SCADA, and OT security
- Satellite and timing dependency risk
- Signal and RF trust assumptions
- Cyber hygiene, asset discovery, and defensive visibility
- Python scripting and security tooling
- Critical infrastructure protection
